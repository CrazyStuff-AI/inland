Featured Town

Antequera, Malaga

Known as "the heart of Andalucia" due to its central location, Antequera is a historic town with rich architectural heritage, surrounded by beautiful countryside and the unique El Torcal nature reserve. With excellent transport connections, it's a perfect base to explore the region.

  • Central location with good transport links
  • Rich cultural heritage and local cuisine
  • Affordable property prices compared to coastal areas
